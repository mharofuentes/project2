Different types of Hamster Breeds/h1>

Syrian Hamster

Traits: unsociable, calm, nocturnal,likes to be alone

Average lifespan: 2-3 years

Average size: around 3-4 inches

Education

Chinese Hamster

Traits: quick,playful,high maintenance,hyperactive

Average lifespan: 2-3 years

Average size: 3-4 inches

Roborovski Hamster

Traits: sociable, fast, selfish, independent, like to live with other hamsters

Average lifespan:2.5-3 years

Average size: 2 inches

Cambell Russian Hamster

Traits: shy, affectionate, likes extremely quiet places, like hiding

Average lifespan: 1.5-2 years

Average size: 2-4 years

Hmaster housing

  • All species of hamsters like big and spacious environment. Wired cages are bad. A big plastic container is better.
  • Containers should be atleast 24 inches by 12 inches. Lots of places to hide is recommended. With fresh food and water daily.
